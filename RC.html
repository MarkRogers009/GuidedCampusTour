ReyRey Café

The ReyRey Café is located inside Rike Hall. The cafe was sponsored by the Reynolds and Reynolds Foundation and first opened on December 2, 2015, and it is Wright State's first student-operated cafe. The cafe is open Monday to Thursday from 8 am to 5 pm; it is also open Friday from 8:30 am to 3 pm, and it is closed on Saturday and Sunday. The cafe serves Boston Stoker coffee and also carries Pepsi products, pastries, and snacks.
