The Hangar

The Hangar is located inside Allyn Hall. The Hangar is a food court that is open from Monday to Friday from 7:30 am to 10 pm. Breakfast is from 7:30 to 10 am, and lunch is from 10 am to 3 pm. The Hangar is also open from Saturday to Sunday from 10 am to 6 pm with a limited menu. Dining options include Burger Barn, Asada, Blend, and Bite.
