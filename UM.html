Union Market

Union Market is located inside Student Union. The Union Market is the All You Care to Eat (AYCTE) dining hall. The Union Market has grilled food, wood-fired pizzas, made-to-order stir-fry, and international cuisine. The dining hall also includes a comfort foods bar and a salad bar. The dining hall is open Monday to Friday from 11 am to 4 pm. The AYCTE price includes one re-entry during the same meal period. The AYCTE is dine-in only with the exception of the Raider Green To-Go Container program.
